I'm Computational-Biologist

Abdul is a graduate student in the Department of Bioinformatics at Government College University Faisalabad, currently pursuing a bachelor's degree in the subject. Abdul's primary area of expertise is computational skills in the analysis of Genomics, Proteomics, and Transcriptomics data using various Bioinformatics Approaches. He also works as an instructor at Udemy, where he teaches bioinformatics to learners worldwide. Abdul has hands-on experience in LINUX OS, Python, and R scripting. His research interests are centered on genome-wide analysis of gene families, particularly their role in abiotic stress. He has published several research papers in high-impact factor journals related to this topic.
